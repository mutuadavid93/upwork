The CP, or preparatory course, is the first class of elementary school whose priority objectives are to allow students to learn to read, write and count. From CP, students also discover the learning of a modern language (foreign or regional), lessons to question the world, artistic lessons (plastic arts and musical education), physical and sports education and moral and civic.
